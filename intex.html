About FactGuard AI

Understanding fake news and how our AI detection system works

What is Fake News?

Fake news refers to deliberately fabricated or misleading information presented as legitimate news. It includes:

  • Fabricated Content: Completely made-up stories with no factual basis
  • Manipulated Content: Real information altered to deceive
  • Misleading Content: Information presented out of context
  • Imposter Content: Fake sources pretending to be legitimate

Why Detection Matters

In today's digital age, misinformation can spread rapidly and have serious consequences:

📊 Democracy

Fake news can influence elections and undermine democratic processes

🏥 Public Health

Medical misinformation can lead to harmful health decisions

💰 Financial

False news can manipulate markets and cause financial harm

🤝 Social Trust

Misinformation erodes trust in media and institutions
